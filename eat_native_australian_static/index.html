Native Australian Plants are delicious

Healthy and Tasty

Australian Native foods contain vast amounts of antioxidants and vitamins. Did you know that the Kakadu plum has more Vitamins in it then any other fruit in the world?

Great for the Environment

Unlike their European counterparts, native foods do not require large amounts of fertilizer to grow. This is thanks to the harsh Australian environment.

Indigenous Communities

The Indigenous communities have known about the health enchancing properties of Native foods for thousands of years and have a vast amount of expertise in their cultivation.
